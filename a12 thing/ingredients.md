# Ingredients(for 4 people)
Two packs of ramen noodles of your choice.
5 cups of pork or beef broth (You can use the stuff in the package as a substitute.)
Pork Tenderloin
2 Eggs
1 Baby bok choy
1 Green Onion
Soy Sauce
Aburage x5
Nori
Kamaboko x3
all this to make naruto style ramen
